Glowscape Planter

Difficulty: Beginner
Time: 10-20 minutes

Materials You'll Need

Glass Jar

Holds all the elements together.

Rocks

Add weight and texture.

LED Light String

Creates a warm glow.

String

Ties around the jar for decoration.

Plant

Adds a touch of greenery.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Prepare Your Jar

Make sure the glass jar is clean and dry before you begin.

2

Add the LED Light String

Place the LED light string inside the jar first. Coil it loosely to cover the base and spread the light evenly.

3

Add Rocks

Pour a layer of small decorative rocks into the jar. This helps to secure the light string and adds a natural texture.

4

Insert the Plant

Carefully place a small plant (real or artificial) on top of the rocks. Center it so it stands upright and is visible.

5

Turn On the Lights

Switch on the LED string lights. Your Glow Jar is now complete and ready to light up your space!
